## 📚 Curriculum Topics Covered

### Primary 6 Mathematics
1. **Numbers & Operations** - Whole numbers, fractions, decimals, percentages
2. **Measurement** - Length, area, volume, time, money, temperature
3. **Geometry** - 2D shapes, angles, symmetry, basic transformations
4. **Data Handling** - Charts, graphs, tables, averages, mode, median
5. **Problem Solving** - Word problems, logical reasoning, patterns
6. **Ratio & Proportion** - Basic ratios, proportional relationships

### Primary 7 Mathematics
1. **Advanced Numbers** - Large numbers, complex fractions, scientific notation
2. **Algebra Basics** - Simple equations, variables, expressions
3. **3D Geometry** - Solid shapes, surface area, volume calculations
4. **Advanced Statistics** - Probability, data analysis, interpretation
5. **Financial Mathematics** - Interest, profit/loss, budgeting, taxation
6. **Advanced Problem Solving** - Multi-step problems, real-world applications
